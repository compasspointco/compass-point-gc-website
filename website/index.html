Services

Purpose-built IT capabilities for federal and state government missions.

01

IT Staff Augmentation

Place cleared, experienced technical professionals into agency teams on a contract basis — program managers, systems engineers, cloud architects, and security specialists ready to contribute from day one.

Who we serve: Program offices, prime contractors, and agencies with staffing requirements under SDVOSB set-aside vehicles.

Outcome: Mission-capable staff integrated quickly without the overhead of permanent hires — on the timeline agencies actually need.

02

Systems Design & Integration

Architect and assemble interoperable technology systems built to agency requirements — emphasizing clean interfaces, testable designs, and maintainable deployments that reduce long-term cost and risk.

Who we serve: IT program leads, system integrators, and agencies standing up new capabilities.

Outcome: Systems that work as specified, documented to standard, and built for the teams who will own them after delivery.

03

Cloud Migration & Infrastructure

Plan and execute cloud migrations to AWS GovCloud, Azure Government, or Google Cloud — with secure, compliant infrastructure baselines, proper data classification handling, and knowledge transfer built in.

Who we serve: Agencies modernizing legacy applications, data centers, and infrastructure under cloud mandates.

Outcome: Cloud environments that meet FedRAMP and agency security requirements, with a path the mission team can sustain independently.

04

Cybersecurity & Compliance

Security assessments, CMMC advisory services, and incident response readiness — delivered by professionals who understand both the technical requirements and the compliance frameworks agencies operate within.

Who we serve: Security teams, acquisition shops, DIB suppliers, and primes managing subcontractor CMMC compliance.

Outcome: Improved security posture, audit-ready documentation, and a clear roadmap to CMMC Level 2 or 3 certification.

05

IT Project & Program Management

Disciplined project and program management for complex IT implementations — schedule development, risk management, status reporting, and stakeholder alignment delivered with the clarity government clients require.

Who we serve: Program managers and contracting officers who need accountable delivery, not status theater.

Outcome: Programs that stay on schedule, surface real risks early, and give leadership the visibility to make decisions — not surprises at the end.

About Compass Point

Compass Point LLC was founded by a U.S. military veteran who grew up in Parker, Colorado, served his country, built a career in technology, and came home to build something lasting. We bring the same discipline, precision, and accountability that defines military service to every IT engagement we take on — because government clients deserve a partner who treats their mission like their own.

Michael Petty — Founder

Michael is a U.S. military veteran and senior technology professional with deep experience designing and delivering IT systems for complex organizations. After years in technology — across the private sector and in support of government clients — he returned to Aurora, Colorado to build a firm that reflects his values: rigorous, direct, and committed to doing the work right.

Compass Point is intentionally small by design. Michael works directly with every client. There are no layers of account managers between you and the person accountable for delivery. When a commitment is made, it is kept. When something changes, you hear it from leadership directly.

Mission & Values

  • Mission first. Government clients have missions that matter. We treat that seriously.
  • Accountability without excuses. If a commitment is made, it is kept. If something changes, you hear it from leadership directly.
  • Precision over promises. We scope what we can deliver and deliver what we scope.
  • Colorado roots, national reach. Locally grounded, with the capability to serve agencies and partners across the country.
